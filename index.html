What is CoolSculpting and How Does it Work

CoolSculpting Lubbock is an innovative, non-invasive body contouring treatment designed to eliminate stubborn fat that resists diet and exercise. The treatment uses a process called cryolipolysis, which involves applying controlled cooling to target and freeze fat cells in problem areas. These fat cells are then naturally processed and removed by the body over time, without affecting surrounding tissues.

CoolSculpting targets common trouble spots such as the abdomen, thighs, love handles, and under the chin, helping to sculpt and tone the body. The procedure is quick, typically lasting 35-60 minutes per session, and requires no downtime, so you can return to your daily activities immediately. What to Expect During a CoolSculpting Session

When you choose CoolSculpting Lubbock, understanding what happens during your session can help you feel more at ease.
